Case Study

Rebalance Chiropractic

Helping a growing chiropractic business in Abingdon strengthen its online presence through website support, paid ads and more consistent digital marketing.

Rebalance Chiropractic is run by Rob in Abingdon, near Oxford. The aim was to help the business look more professional online, become more visible locally, and generate more enquiries through a joined-up digital marketing approach across the website, paid ads and organic social media.

Project details

Industry

Chiropractic / Healthcare

Client

Rebalance Chiropractic

Involvement

Website Support, Meta Ads, Google PPC, Organic Social Media

Focus

Local visibility, enquiries, brand professionalism

The brief

The goal was to help Rebalance Chiropractic attract more local attention and convert that visibility into real enquiries from people in and around Abingdon. That meant improving how the brand appeared online and making sure potential clients could discover the business more easily through both paid and organic channels.

The work needed to feel clear, professional and consistent, giving Rob a stronger digital presence that reflected the quality of the service while also helping bring in new local clients.

How we helped

Support for Rebalance Chiropractic covered several key areas of digital marketing, giving the business a more complete and effective online setup. This included work on the website, Meta ads, Google PPC campaigns, and organic social media templates alongside weekly post support.

By approaching the brand across multiple channels rather than treating each area in isolation, we helped create a stronger, more professional online presence that made it easier for local people to find the business and feel confident getting in touch.

  • Website support to improve the online presence
  • Meta ads setup and management
  • Google PPC campaign support for local visibility
  • Organic social media templates and weekly content support

The outcome

The result was greater visibility for Rebalance Chiropractic and a more polished brand presence online. Rob saw more enquiries coming through and gained many new clients, with local visibility playing a particularly important role in helping the business grow.

With stronger positioning on Google, more professional-looking social content and paid campaigns supporting awareness and enquiry generation, the business was better placed to reach the right people in the local area.

“The aim was to make the business easier to find, stronger in presentation, and more effective at turning online visibility into genuine local enquiries.”

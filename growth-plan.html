What it is

Marketing help that keeps things moving.

The Piechart Growth Plan is for businesses that do not need a huge agency package, but do need a reliable pair of hands each month.

It is designed for useful, practical support. That might mean updating a website, reviewing a campaign, improving a landing page, checking your tracking, helping with social content ideas, or giving you a clearer plan for what to do next.

It is not unlimited support. The base plan includes a set monthly allowance, so the scope stays clear and manageable.

FAQs

A few things worth knowing.

The Growth Plan is designed to be useful and clear. The aim is to avoid vague promises and keep the monthly support easy to understand.

How much support is included?

The base Growth Plan includes up to 2 hours of digital marketing support per month. That time can be used across website updates, ads advice, content input, tracking checks and practical marketing support.

Does unused time roll over?

No. The monthly allowance does not roll over. This keeps the plan simple, predictable and easier to manage.

Does this include ad spend?

No. Any ad spend for Meta, Google or other platforms is separate and paid directly by you to the advertising platform.

Does this include full ads management?

No, not as standard. The base plan can include light campaign checks and advice, but full paid ads management would be quoted separately.

Can you help if my website was not built by Piechart?

Usually, yes. We would need to check how the site is built and what access is available, but we can normally advise on the best route.

What if I need more than 2 hours per month?

No problem. We can quote a larger monthly support package based on the level of help you need.

Is this only for Manchester businesses?

No. We are based in Greater Manchester and love working locally, but we can support businesses across the UK.

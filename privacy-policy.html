Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12th April 2026

Piechart Digital is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information in a transparent and responsible way. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and store your data when you visit our website, contact us, or work with us.

1. Who we are

Piechart Digital is a Manchester-based performance marketing studio. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can contact us at hello@piechartdigital.co.uk.

2. What information we collect

We may collect and process the following types of personal information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business details you choose to provide
  • Website usage data, such as pages visited and browser/device information
  • Any other information you send to us through forms, email or other contact methods

3. How we collect your information

We may collect information when you:

  • Fill in a contact form on our website
  • Email us directly
  • Use or browse our website
  • Engage with us as a client, supplier or partner

4. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to enquiries
  • Provide services and communicate about ongoing work
  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Maintain records of communications and projects
  • Meet legal, regulatory or contractual obligations

5. Legal basis for processing

We only process personal data where we have a lawful basis to do so. Depending on the situation, this may include:

  • Your consent
  • Performance of a contract
  • Compliance with a legal obligation
  • Legitimate interests, where these are not overridden by your rights and interests

6. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers where necessary to operate our website, deliver services, or manage business operations. These providers are only given access to the information they need and are expected to handle it securely.

7. How long we keep your data

We keep personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose it was collected, including to satisfy legal, accounting or reporting requirements.

8. Cookies and website tracking

Our website currently uses only essential cookies that are necessary for the site to function properly. These cookies do not collect personal data for marketing or analytics purposes. We may introduce optional cookies in the future for purposes such as website analytics or advertising. Where this happens, you will be given a clear choice to accept or reject these cookies before they are used. You can also control cookies through your browser settings at any time.

9. Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from unauthorised access, loss, misuse, disclosure or alteration. However, no online system can be guaranteed completely secure.

10. Your rights

Depending on your location and the circumstances, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent where consent is the basis for processing
  • Request transfer of your data where applicable

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@piechartdigital.co.uk.

11. Third-party links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the content, privacy practices or security of those external sites.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the latest version will always apply.

13. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the way we handle your personal data, please contact:

Piechart Digital
Email: hello@piechartdigital.co.uk

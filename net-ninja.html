Case Study

Net Ninja

Supporting one of the best-known coding education brands with paid ads, email marketing and practical digital support built around growth.

Net Ninja has built a strong reputation through clear teaching, high quality content and a recognisable brand in the coding space. Our role was to support that growth with paid ads, campaign thinking and digital activity that helped drive more awareness, more sign-ups and stronger performance from key landing pages and offers.

Project details

Industry

Education / Online Learning

Client

Net Ninja

Involvement

Paid Ads, Email Marketing, Landing Page Support

Focus

Lead generation, course promotion, audience growth

The brief

The aim was to support Net Ninja with paid digital activity that could help bring the right people into the funnel, promote premium course offers, and strengthen the wider digital journey around sign-ups and conversions.

The work needed to feel consistent with the existing brand, avoid overcomplication, and focus on practical improvements that could drive measurable value rather than vanity metrics.

How we helped

Our support focused on creating and managing paid campaigns built around clear offers, relevant audiences and clean conversion journeys. That meant thinking carefully about messaging, creative, targeting and where traffic was being sent.

Alongside ad activity, we also supported wider digital marketing areas including email marketing and landing page thinking, helping make sure the journey after the click felt just as considered as the ad itself.

  • Paid social campaign setup and management
  • Creative and messaging support for course promotion
  • Email marketing input around offers and audience engagement
  • Landing page and conversion journey support

The outcome

The result was a more joined-up digital marketing approach, with paid activity supporting wider commercial goals rather than operating in isolation. The focus stayed on attracting the right users, improving campaign efficiency and making the overall journey feel clearer and stronger.

For a brand like Net Ninja, where trust, clarity and consistency matter, that joined-up thinking is what helps paid activity work harder.

“The goal was not just to drive traffic, but to support meaningful growth through better campaigns, stronger messaging and a cleaner route to conversion.”

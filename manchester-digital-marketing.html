Looking for digital marketing in Manchester?

If you are searching for a digital marketing agency in Manchester, you probably do not just want someone to “do some marketing”. You want clearer leads, better visibility, stronger creative, a website that does its job and campaigns that are actually connected to your business goals.

That is where Piechart Digital fits. We help businesses make better use of the digital channels that matter most, from Meta ads and Google PPC to landing pages, social media content and campaign strategy.

We are not interested in making marketing feel more complicated than it needs to be. Our approach is simple: understand the offer, sharpen the message, build the right journey and keep improving based on what is actually happening.

Paid ads for your business in Manchester

Paid ads can be a brilliant way to generate enquiries, but only when the foundations are right. The targeting, creative, copy, landing page and tracking all need to work together.

We support businesses with Facebook ads, Instagram ads, Google Search campaigns, retargeting, lead generation campaigns and campaign audits. Whether you are starting from scratch or need someone to improve what is already running, we can help make your ad spend work harder.

Websites and landing pages that support growth

Getting traffic is only half the job. If the page people land on is confusing, slow or unclear, your marketing will always struggle.

We build websites and landing pages with clear messaging, strong calls to action and a structure designed to turn visitors into leads. This is especially important if you are spending money on paid ads, because every wasted click costs money.

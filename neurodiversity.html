Neurodiversity

Clear communication.
Flexible support.

We want Piechart Digital to feel easy to work with. That means being clear, practical and open to different ways of communicating and collaborating.

A more comfortable way of working

We know that not everyone works, thinks, processes information or communicates in the same way. For some people, overly vague messaging, rushed calls, unclear expectations or too much back and forth can make working relationships harder than they need to be.

That is why we try to keep things straightforward. We aim to communicate clearly, set out what is happening, explain what we need from you, and avoid creating confusion where it can be avoided. We would rather be practical and helpful than overly polished and hard to follow.

For us, being neurodivergent-friendly is not about turning it into a big corporate statement. It is about making sensible adjustments where we can, being respectful, and creating a working style that feels calmer, clearer and more manageable.

Clear communication Flexible contact Less jargon Practical support

If there is a certain way you prefer to work, you are very welcome to tell us. That might be email over phone, more detail before decisions are made, clearer written summaries, fewer meetings, extra time to process things, or just a more direct and structured approach. None of that is a problem.

What this can look like

  • Email-first communication where possible
  • Clear written follow-ups after key discussions
  • Less jargon and fewer fluffy explanations
  • Structured plans broken into manageable steps
  • Flexibility around pace and communication style

Need something specific?

We are happy to adapt where we reasonably can. Just let us know what helps, and we will do our best to work in a way that suits you.

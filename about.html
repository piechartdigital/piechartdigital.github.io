Who we are

Practical digital marketing, without the fluff.

Piechart Digital was built around a simple idea: small businesses deserve clear, creative and effective digital marketing support without being buried in jargon, confusing retainers or overcomplicated agency processes.

We help businesses turn their online presence into something that works harder. That might mean running better paid ads, improving a landing page, creating stronger social content, building a clearer website, or giving you a sensible plan for what to focus on next.

We are based in Greater Manchester, with a strong local focus, but we work with businesses across the UK. Whether you are a trade business, a local service provider, a growing brand or a small business owner trying to get more leads online, the aim is always the same: clear thinking, strong execution and marketing that supports real business goals.

Founder-led support

Led by digital marketing experience.

Piechart is led by Jay Perrin, a digital marketer with more than 15 years of experience across paid social, content, landing pages, websites, email marketing and campaign strategy.

Over the years, Jay has worked across a wide mix of businesses, from local brands and family-run companies to national campaigns, education platforms, insurance providers, sports organisations and specialist service businesses.

The focus now is simple: help businesses make better use of their online presence, get clearer on what they are trying to achieve and put digital marketing activity in place that actually supports growth.

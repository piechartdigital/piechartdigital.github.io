About Piechart

Small studio energy. Big agency standards.

Piechart Digital is a Manchester-based digital marketing studio helping small and growing businesses get more from their websites, ads, content and online presence.

Jay & Madeline Founders

Practical digital marketing, without the fluff.

Piechart Digital was built around a simple idea: small businesses deserve bespoke, creative and effective digital marketing support without being buried in jargon or overcomplicated agency processes.

We help businesses turn their online presence into something more refined, that works as hard as possible for them and their specific needs. That might mean running better paid ads, improving a landing page, creating stronger social content, building a clearer website, or giving you a sensible plan for what to focus on next.

We are based in Greater Manchester, with a strong local focus, but we work with businesses across the UK. Whether you are a trade business, a local service provider, a growing brand or a small business owner trying to improve your presence online, the aim is always the same: clear thinking, strong execution and marketing that supports your specific business goals.
